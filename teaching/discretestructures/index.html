Course Topics

Topics 1-6 are covered in EDAA40 and EDAA75 and the two additional topics are only covered in EDAA75.

1. Proofs and propositional logic

Book chapters: Chapters 1 and 3.1-3.5

Homework: a1_proof.ipynb.zip

Proof techniques, mathematical reasoning, and writing clear formal arguments.

2. Predicate formulas and sets

Book chapters: Chapters 3.6 and and 4.1

Homework: a2_predicates-and-sets.ipynb.zip

Propositional logic, predicate logic, quantifiers, formulas, and logical equivalence.

3. Relations and functions

Book chapters: Chapter 4.2-4.5

Homework: a3_functions_and_relations.zip

Sets, set operations, relations, equivalence relations, partial orders, and functions.

3.5 Well-ordering principle and infinities

Book chapters: Chapters 2 and 8

No homework

4. Induction and recursion

Book chapters: Chapters 5 and 7.3

Homework: a4-induction-and-recursion.ipynb.zip

Mathematical induction, strong induction, recursive definitions, and recurrence relations.

5. Graphs, trees, orders

Book chapters: Chapter 10

Homework: a5-graphs-and-trees.zip

Basic graph concepts, trees, ordered structures, and related discrete models.

6. Number theory

Book chapters: Chapter 9.1-9.7

Homework: a6-number-theory.ipynb.zip

Divisibility, modular arithmetic, greatest common divisors, and number-theoretic reasoning.

EDAA75-A: Graph theory

Book chapters: Chapter 12

Homework: a7-graph-theory.zip

Additional graph theory material for EDAA75.

EDAA75-B: Combinatorics

Book chapters: Chapters 14.1-14.3 and 15

Homework: a8-combinatorics.ipynb.zip

Additional combinatorics material for EDAA75.
